About me

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. student in the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Longbo Huang.

I received my B.E. in 2020 at Tsinghua University, major in Electronic Engineering and second major in Economics and Finance.

My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning (Theory, Algorithm and Applications), Generative AI (especially Diffusion Models and Large Language Models) and Network Optimization.
